Company Profile

Durham House of Pets (DHP) was founded by Mr. Williams in 2000. In fact, Mr. Williams's original intention was to provide convenience to his pet-owning neighbors in the community, so early DHP was to sell simple dog and cat food. But Mr. Williams's reputation and conscientious price have attracted more and more customers.
Over a decade DHP has grown into a household pet store in Durham. Now we not only sell all kinds of pet food, but also sell pet clothes, toys and fish tanks.

Why Choose Us

Providing the best products has always been our slogan.
Here we will help you choose the best food for your pet,
as well as a variety of popular pet clothing.
Our pet toys will also bring more fun to your pets.
Most importantly,
as an enterprise growing up in our hometown,
we are committed to providing our neighbors
with the cheapest price and the best products.
